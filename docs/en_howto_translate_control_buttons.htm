How to translate control buttons

The iso code for simplified chinese = "zh" will be used for the examples below. You can also use the chinese traditional "zh-tw" if necessary.

Other language codes are listed at the bottom of this page.

Step 1. Create a new directory "zh" inside the /gui/img/control/default/ directory.

Step 2. Copy all button images from /gui/img/control/default/en/ to /gui/img/control/default/zh/

Step 3. Rename the copied files by replacing the "en_" part of the filename with "zh_" .

Step 4. Start your graphic editor program and edit each button replacing the english word with the word in your language.

    en_register_green.gif zh_register_green.gif
    >>>>>


    en_employment_blue.gif zh_employment_blue.gif
    >>>>>

What to do next?

You can send all image files as zip or tar.gz to admin(at)care2x.com everytime you finished a batch of images or you can upload the images to the project's CVS (not compressed) and notify admin(at)care2x.com.

OK, I've finished all button images. What to do next?

Send a colored photo of yours with a short info about yourself (in your language and in english) to admin(at)care2x.com. We will add you to the credits page.

OK, everything sent. What to do next?

Nothing more. Use and enjoy your new Care2x.
